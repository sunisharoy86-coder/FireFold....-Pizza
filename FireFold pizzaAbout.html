Our Story

A Family Tradition Since 1985

FireFold Pizza was founded by Carmelo Rossi, who brought his family's authentic Neapolitan pizza recipe from Naples, Italy. What started as a small pizzeria has grown into a beloved local institution.

For over three decades, we've been serving the community with the same passion and dedication that Carmelo started with. Every pizza is still made by hand, using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

Today, Carmelo's daughter Maria leads our kitchen, ensuring every pizza that leaves our wood-fired oven honors the family legacy and authentic Italian flavor that made us who we are.
